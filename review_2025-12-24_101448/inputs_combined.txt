ASTM E1264 - Standard Classification for Acoustical Ceiling Products.

ASTM E84 - Standard Test Method for Surface Burning Characteristics of Building Materials.

SUBMITTALS

Product Data: Submit manufacturer's technical data for each product.

Include performance characteristics and installation instructions.

Include ceiling panel patterns and edge details.

Samples: Submit two full-size samples of each ceiling panel type.

Shop Drawings: Submit layout drawings showing panel arrangement and edge conditions.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

Fire Performance: Provide panels with Class A fire rating per ASTM E84.

Seismic Requirements: Comply with ASCE 7 and CBC Chapter 13 for ceiling systems.

PART 2 - PRODUCTS

MANUFACTURERS

Acceptable Manufacturers:

Armstrong World Industries.

USG Corporation.

CertainTeed Corporation.

Substitutions: In accordance with Section 01 60 00.

ACOUSTIC CEILING PANELS

Standard Acoustic Panel (Type ACT-1):

Size: 24 inches by 24 inches by 3/4 inch thick.

NRC: 0.55 minimum.

CAC: 35 minimum.

Light Reflectance: 0.83 minimum.

Edge Detail: Square lay-in.

Color: White.

High-NRC Acoustic Panel (Type ACT-2):

Size: 24 inches by 48 inches by 3/4 inch thick.

NRC: 0.70 minimum.

CAC: 35 minimum.

Edge Detail: Tegular.

Application: Conference rooms and private offices.

SUSPENSION SYSTEM

Grid System: Heavy-duty exposed tee system per ASTM C635.

Main Runners: 15/16 inch face width.

Cross Tees: 15/16 inch face width, intermediate duty.

Finish: White baked enamel to match ceiling panels.

Hanger Wire: 12 gauge galvanized steel wire.

Seismic Bracing: As required by code for Seismic Design Category D.

PART 3 - EXECUTION

EXAMINATION

Verify conditions are ready for ceiling installation:

Structure is complete and accessible.

MEP systems above ceiling are installed and tested.

Room temperature and humidity are within manufacturer's limits.

Do not proceed until unsatisfactory conditions are corrected.

INSTALLATION

Install suspension system per ASTM C636 and manufacturer's instructions.

Install hanger wires at maximum 4 feet on center.

Locate first hanger within 2 feet of each wall.

Provide supplemental hangers at light fixtures and diffusers.

Level grid to within 1/8 inch in 12 feet.

Install seismic bracing per engineered drawings.

Install ceiling panels after grid is complete and inspected.

CLEANING AND PROTECTION

Clean exposed surfaces of ceiling panels and grid.

Replace panels that are damaged, soiled, or do not match.

Protect installed ceilings from damage by other trades.

END OF SECTION

Install valves in horizontal piping with stem at or above center of pipe.

Install valves in position to allow full stem movement.
